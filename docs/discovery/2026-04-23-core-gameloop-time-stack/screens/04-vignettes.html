How does Ronki carry time? — four weekly vignettes

Same idea (a weekly reflection beat), four different shapes. Each scales differently to month/season/journey. Pick one — the others become wrong-shaped at scale.

SONNTAG MORGEN · LAGERFEUER
🐉
"Mein Buch ist ein bisschen voller geworden — willst du sehen?"

A — "Sonntag bei Ronki"

Cadence: fixed (Sunday). Initiation: Ronki invites. Surface: opens Buch.

Sunday morning, Ronki has a calm pose at the fire and a single quip. Tap → opens a new weekly page in the Buch where Ronki narrates 3-5 highlights from the week. Voice + text. Predictable rhythm.

Scales to: monthly chapter close (last Sunday of month), seasonal volume, journey farewell.

SONNTAG · FEUER WÄCHST
🔥
3 Steine diese Woche · tippe einen

B — "Die Woche brennt im Feuer"

Cadence: continuous. Initiation: kid discovers. Surface: Lagerfeuer scene.

A small physical mark appears each week (a stone added to the fire ring per milestone, or a sapling near the camp). Kid notices when ready. Tap a stone → Ronki tells you what it stands for.

Scales to: month = bigger object (a tent, a path), season = scenery shift (snow, blossoms), journey = a whole settled camp.

IRGENDWANN DIE WOCHE · "REMEMBERING"
🐲
"Ich hab gerade an Donnerstag gedacht…"

C — "Ronki-Erinnerung" (jederzeit)

Cadence: floating (1-2× per week, random day). Initiation: Ronki + on-demand pull. Surface: Lagerfeuer quip → mini reflection.

No fixed Sunday. Ronki has a "remembering" face once or twice a week and a single quip. Kid can also pull anytime via a permanent "Erzähl mir was" tile near the fire.

Scales to: month = "Ich erinnere mich an viele Tage…" / season = scenic shift cue / journey = farewell as a long final remembering.

SONNTAG NACHT · STILL
📖
Eine neue Seite ist im Buch.
— keine Mitteilung, keine Glocke

D — "Stille Buchseite"

Cadence: fixed (Sunday night). Initiation: none — purely discovered. Surface: Buch (silent).

Ronki doesn't say anything. The Buch silently gains a new page. The kid finds it next time they open the Buch. The most "fades by design" option — also the most invisible.

Scales to: same pattern, just bigger pages. Risks landing as no-op for kids who rarely open Buch.

The axis you're really picking

A and D are fixed Sunday. B and C are floating / discovered. A and C are Ronki invites. B and D are kid finds. The combinatorics matter — A is the most "ritual," B is the most "world," C is the most "relational," D is the most "quiet." There's no wrong answer; there's a shape that matches Ronki's brand spine best.
